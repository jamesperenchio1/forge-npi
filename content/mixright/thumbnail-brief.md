# MixRight — Thumbnail Brief

## Concept A (Recommended)
**Split frame:**
- Left half: cracked concrete floor (gray, depressing, labeled "before: guessing")
- Right half: MixRight app open showing big orange "20.2L" number (labeled "after: math")
- Large centered text: "THE CONCRETE CALCULATOR THAT KNOWS IT'S RAINING"
- Text color: white with thin black outline for readability on both halves

## Concept B
**Single frame:**
- Phone propped against a cement bag, app showing results
- Big yellow text overlay: "WHY IS THERE WATER IN MY CONCRETE FORMULA"
- Sub-text: "BECAUSE IT'S 88% HUMIDITY OUTSIDE"
- Font: black bold, slightly too large, slightly crooked (intentional)

## Concept C (most meme-able)
**Two-panel meme format:**
- Top panel: YT engineer saying "adjust for ambient conditions" (screenshot or recreation)
- Bottom panel: MixRight app with arrow pointing at it saying "this."

## Platform notes
- TikTok/Reels: Concept A or C — big text, reads in 1 second
- YouTube Shorts: Concept B — slightly longer dwell time
- All: test both dark and light background versions — construction sites have bright light

## Colors to use
- Orange: #C2541F (matches app primary)
- Concrete gray: #8C8680
- White + black text only, no other colors
