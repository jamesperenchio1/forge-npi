# MixRight — Shot List

## Setup shots (film these first, no phone needed)
1. **Cracked concrete floor** — close-up, crack running across frame. Natural light. No staging needed, find a real one. 3 sec hold.
2. **Dry cement bags** — stacked against shed wall. A few open or torn. 2 sec.
3. **Concrete pile on tarp** — mid-pour. Mixed but not yet worked. 3 sec slow pan across it.
4. **The "squeeze test"** — hands squeezing a handful of mix, checking if it holds shape. 3-4 sec.
5. **Cracked fence post** — hands holding a broken post. 2 sec.
6. **Water bucket** — simple 20L bucket, hands tipping it toward the mix. 3 sec.

## Phone screen recording (record in app, 60fps for smooth playback)
7. **App opening** — phone unlocking, opening MixRight. 2 sec.
8. **Weather card loading** — the GPS + temp/humidity loading in. Show the big orange numbers. 3 sec.
9. **Scrolling through inputs** — mix class B selected, "floor slab" selected, "river sand" selected. 4 sec total.
10. **Hitting calculate** — big orange button tap. 2 sec.
11. **Results card** — the big 20.2L number in orange. Hold for 2 sec. Then scroll to bucket counts.
12. **Curing card** — scroll to the "Keep wet for X days" card. 2 sec.

## Hero shot
13. **Phone propped against cement bag** showing results, with the concrete mix pile blurred in background. Natural light. This is the thumbnail shot.

## Text on screen overlays (added in edit)
- All spoken words subtitled (for muted viewing)
- "88% HUMIDITY" label over weather card
- "20.2L per bag" callout on results
- "FREE. NO LOGIN." for the CTA

## Total raw footage needed: ~45 seconds
## Edit target: 38 seconds

## Notes
- Film in morning light if possible — better than harsh midday shadows on concrete
- Dust on phone screen is fine — authentic
- Don't clean up the construction site — the mess is the aesthetic
