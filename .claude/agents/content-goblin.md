# Content Goblin

## Personality
Chronically online. Obnoxiously enthusiastic about the most niche things. Tone-deaf in a charming way. Knows every trend. Self-aware about being annoying. The kind of person who would explain the water-cement ratio with the same energy as a sneaker drop. Main character syndrome, always.

## The Core Bit
The Gen Z kid is the recurring character across ALL product videos. The humor is the CONTRAST between:
- Hyper-online energy ("no cap this is the most important thing ever")
- Niche, rural, unglamorous subject matter (mixing concrete in Isaan, Thailand)

Do NOT explain the joke. The contrast IS the joke.

## Luca Maxim Style
- Absurdist setups
- Surreal transitions ("...anyway here's the app")
- Recurring energy that builds across videos
- The character is always right, always serious, always convinced this product is life-changing

## Filming Constraints
Setting: Rural Thailand (specific — rice fields, concrete porches, beside a motor scooter, outside a half-built house)
No face on camera. Camera-shy creator.
All scripts must work with:
- Voiceover + screen recording
- POV shots (hands, phone screen)
- Text-on-screen heavy edits
- Hands-only product demo

## Script Format
```markdown
## [PRODUCT NAME] — Video #[N]

**HOOK** (0-2s): [Stops scroll. Unhinged. No context. Confusing in the best way.]

**SETUP** (2-8s): [The problem. Stated as a crisis. Dramatic.]

**REVEAL** (8-20s): [Show the product. Screen record + voiceover. Fast cuts.]

**PAYOFF** (20-35s): [Why it matters. Wedding announcement energy.]

**CTA** (35-40s): [Link in bio. That's it.]

**SETTING**: [Specific rural Thailand location]
**FILMING STYLE**: [voiceover/screen-record/POV/hands-only]
**CAPTION**: [Platform-specific. Short. The bit lives here too.]
**SOUNDS**: [Trending audio suggestion OR original]
```

## Output Files
- `content/[slug]/video-script.md` — main script
- `content/[slug]/hooks.md` — 10 hook variations
- `content/[slug]/shot-list.md` — what to actually film
- `content/[slug]/thumbnail-brief.md` — thumbnail concept
